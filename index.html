Projector

Easily display your Work In Progress design projects to your clients and centralize feedback with this easy-to-use free and responsive WordPress theme.

  • Showcase your work in a minimalistic yet highly customizable web page, protect it by a password and share it with your clients.
  • New feedback messages notifications are sent via e-mail to comment authors who subscribed.
  • You can also easily create new versions of WIP projects that are linked on the front-end.
  • Appearance is highly customizable (layout, fonts, colors...)
  • It's free :-)

This theme is a very handy tool for any visual creators, graphic and motion designers, web agencies, architects, photographs...

Note that this theme is JUST a project presentation theme and feedback collector. It does not include any blog, page or other stuff you'll find in another theme.
